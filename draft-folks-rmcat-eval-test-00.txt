2.  Terminology

   The terminology defined in RTP [RFC3550], RTP Profile for Audio and

   Report (XR) [RFC3611], Extended RTP Profile for RTCP-based Feedback
   (RTP/AVPF) [RFC4585] and Support for Reduced-Size RTCP [RFC5506]
   apply.
